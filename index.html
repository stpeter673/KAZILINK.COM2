Who We Are

Rooted in Faith,
Committed to Community

BIDII is a Christian-based, non-profit organisation registered in Kenya, dedicated to building community capacity and improving livelihoods across vulnerable and underserved populations.

With headquarters in Machakos County, we extend our reach across multiple counties — bringing together people of goodwill to share talent, time, and treasure in service of a greater purpose.

We believe that lasting change must come from within communities themselves. Every initiative we undertake is designed to empower people and cultivate resilience, not dependency.

Why Community
Comes First

At BIDII, we don't just deliver services — we walk alongside communities, building trust and cultivating local leadership that sustains change long after our involvement.

Our holistic approach addresses the interconnected roots of poverty: health, education, economic stability, clean water, governance, and spiritual wellbeing.

Our Promise to Communities

Every programme we run is designed with local voices at its core. We believe that communities are not passive recipients of aid — they are the architects of their own transformation.

BIDII serves as a catalyst and partner, equipping individuals and groups with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to create lasting, generational change.

From Machakos County to the wider region, our presence is felt through healthier families, stronger businesses, cleaner water, and more engaged citizens.
